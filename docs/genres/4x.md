# 4X

**Exemplar games**: Shadow Empire, Master of Orion, Civilization

eXplore, eXpand, eXploit, eXterminate — empire-building strategy.

## Required engine capabilities

- Multi-scale map navigation (planet ↔ region ↔ hex)
- Unit and equipment designer
- Supply and logistics network
- Manufacturing and production queue
- Faction and reputation system
- Diplomacy AI
- Tech tree and research system
- Resource economy
- Administration and zone management
- Procedural world generation (planet gen)
- Region, province, territory map system
- Fog of war and visibility system
- Economic engine
- Skill and attribute system
- AI behaviors
